About Physible

When NFTs took the crypto-verse by storm, I lamented that many beautiful pieces of art being minted as NFTs would forever stay in the metaverse, physically too far to touch and hold. Having been an avid 3D Printing enthusiast for over 7 years now, it was natural for me to hit this nail with the only hammer I had. I kicked up the idea of identifying an NFT that would look beautiful in 3D, then modeling it into a three-dimensional file using CAD software, and then bringing it to life through the art and science of 3D Printing.

Current projects

XScapeNFT's XBlade

XScapeNFT is an amazing XRPL gaming project, that erupted into this space with their love for community and the XBlade NFTs. With only 8888 Blades ever to be minted, they're rare and they're selling out fast. Physible collaborated with the XScape team to bring their amazing blades to life using 3D Printing.

XRPL Gaming Alliance

The XRPL Gaming Alliance is a groundbreaking partnership founded and governed by established XRPL projects with the aim to promote blockchain-enhanced gaming and support the expression and growth of gaming-related projects on the XRP Ledger. Physible is now a proud member of the alliance, and preparing to serve the alliance's many members some 3D printed merch!
